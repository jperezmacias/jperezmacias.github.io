Science Books

  • Timaeus, Plato
  • A brief history of Time, Stephen W. Hawking
  • Historia naturalis, Plinio
  • On the Origin of Species, Charles Darwin
  • The Fractal Geometry of Nature by Benoît Mandelbrot
  • A Treatise on Electricity and Magnetism by James Clerk Maxwell
  • De Revolutionibus Orbium Coelestium by Nicolaus Copernicus
  • De Motu by Galileo Galilei
  • Philosophiæ Naturalis Principia Mathematica by Isaac Newton

  • De Rerum Natura by Lucretius
